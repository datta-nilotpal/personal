How to Petition the Supreme Court of India: Understanding Your Options

If you believe a serious injustice has occurred in your legal matter, you may be considering taking your case to the Supreme Court of India. Understanding how to approach the Supreme Court is crucial. This article explains the key ways to do so: writ petitions, appeals, special leave petitions, transfer of cases, and public interest litigations.

The Supreme Court is the highest judicial authority in India and plays a crucial role in protecting individual rights and ensuring justice. It hears cases where fundamental rights have been violated, legal questions of national importance arise, or serious miscarriages of justice have taken place.
